5Gum Rebrand — 2026

5GUM
REBRAND

As part of a design project, I reimagined 5Gum with full creative freedom - returning to the sensory promise the brand originally made, but never fully delivered. The new and improved 5Gum rebrand really hones in on all of the 5 senses with tactile packaging, and company collabs that truly make “stimulate your senses” come to life. This project included developing entirely new branding, marketing and packaging concepts whilst using AI tools to help visualize and bring them to life.

Brand Identity Packaging Social Media Concept AI Rendering

Overview

Flavour as a sensory system

The 5Gum rebrand reframes the brand around a core idea: flavour isn't just a taste — it's a full sensory experience. Each flavour has its own tactile texture, visual language, and identity system that translates across packaging, social media, and immersive brand moments.

Identity

Feel the Five

The reimagined logo and new trademarked slogan really turn 5Gum's essence into a bold, tactile, and more human identity — transforming the brand from something you simply see into something you truly feel. Feel the Five™

Packaging

The tactile cases

A new look on their already popular gum cases — these ones with texture to emulate every flavour's personality — invite you to not just taste, but truly feel each flavour. With 5 words describing exactly what flavour profile you'll experience, and every surface telling a story, turning a simple chew into a full sensory experience.

The paperback pack

The iconic paperback packs with a sleek new embossed look. Unique and easily transportable. It highlights the five same key words and texture associated which each flavour and sensation profile.

Every flavour gets a signature tactile texture — a visual fingerprint that makes each variant instantly visually and physically recognizable across packaging and digital.

Socials

The Sensational Lock In

The new social media strategy stays true to the raw and real nature of the rebrand. It centers around pushing consumers to lock in during the moments that matter most — at the gym, while studying, or finally getting to those procrastinated chores. It's the perfect time to pop a piece, focus on your surroundings, center yourself, and get into the grind.


The instagram feed brings this focus on this sense of empowerment, highlighting the moments where concentration and presence matter most. At the same time, it celebrates the brand's collaborations and showcases the classic textures that make the experience so distinctive, tying together both the sensory and the routine elements of the rebrand.

Promotional Videos

These are AI concept videos for the promotion of the rebranded products and collaborations. It really shows each texture and product personality, letting viewers see, feel, and imagine the full experience of each piece.

Service Design Uno — 2025

SERVICE
DESIGN UNO

As part of a group project, we were challenged to design a physical card game inspired by the principles of Service Design for Business by Ben Reason. Rather than simply treating learning service design as something purely theoretical, we set out to make it feel practical, engaging, and actually usable.

Service Design Uno is the result — a reimagined take on a nostalgic classic that transforms abstract concepts into something you can actively play with. By blending the familiar gameplay of UNO with real-world thinking, we created an experience that's not only fun, brain teasing, and competitive, but encourages players to actually think like service designers.

Product Design Card Game Service Design Educational Team Project

Overview

THE BRIEF

As part of a team design project, we were tasked with creating a professional-quality, card-based educational game that teaches the comprehensive service design content from Service Design for Business. The game needed to support 3–8 players, run for 30–45 minutes, and be polished enough for a knowledge exchange — where other teams would play our game and we'd play theirs.
